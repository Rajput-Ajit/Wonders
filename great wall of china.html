Great Wall Of China

The Great Wall Of China is one of the most extensive construction projects in history, stretching over 13,000 miles(21,196 kilometers). It was build over several dynaties,with construction starting in the 7th century BC and contunuing into the 16th century. The primary purpose of the Great Wall was not to keep out invading Monglos but to protect against raids and invasion by various nomadic groups and military incursion. The idea that the Great Wall is visible from space with the naked eye is a common misconception.

While it can be seen from low Earth orbit,it is not easly visible without aid. Different chinese dynaties played roles in the construction and maintanance of the wall. Notably,significant portions were build during the Ming Dynasty. The Great Wall is not a continuous wall but consists of multiple walls and fortifications. Different sections were build by Different dynaties and served various strategic purpose. The Great Wall of china has been designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1987.
